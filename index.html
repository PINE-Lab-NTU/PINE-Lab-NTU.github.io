About Us

What are we going to do

Our goal is to build an embodied vision system for autonomous systems to understand the physical world and achieve complex human goals such as household service, industrial assembly and item delivery. The system contains the following techniques:

Interactive Visual Perception that actively collects informative visual clues in large physical world e.g. vision-language navigation, online scene segmentation/detection

General Robotic Manipulation that changes the physical world based on human requirements e.g. visual affordance learning, diffusion policy learning Resource-limited Model Deployment that adapts powerful large foundation models on embodied systems e.g. efficient network training, inference, and compilation.
