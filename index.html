About Us

The goal of Perception and embodied INtElligence (PINE) Lab is to build embodied agents that perceive and interact with the physical world as humans, so that the robot can assist everyone in everyday life to achieve complex human demands. For more information, please visit our research page, or follow us on Github, Youtube and Bilibili.
We are always looking for self-motivated PhD students, postdocs, master students and research assistants. To join our lab, please see the Join Us page.
